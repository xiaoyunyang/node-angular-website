A Revolutionary Tool For Multiplication

Written by Andrew Fenner on August 31, 2014.

Our FlagShip application FortyNine is now available in the iTunes app store. FortyNine gives multiplication tables a bold new look and makes the concept accessible for students at any level. Rediscover the joy of learning with your child in a way that's productive and engaging. No more frustrating worksheets or drills; FortyNine gets right to the point and gives students the edge they need to succeed.
